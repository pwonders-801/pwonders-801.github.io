What are TTRPGs

TTRPGs, also known as tabletop role-playing games, are collaborative storytelling games in which the players role play as characters in a story led by the GM or Game Master. When the outcome of a character's actions are uncertain, dice are rolled according to the game's rule set. Within these rules, players are allowed the freedom to do what they want, and their choices shape how the story develops.
